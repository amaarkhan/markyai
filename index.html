FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What video formats are supported?

Marky supports all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV, and more. You can also paste URLs from YouTube, Vimeo, Wistia, and other video platforms.

How accurate is the transcription?

Our AI-powered transcription has 95%+ accuracy for clear audio in supported languages. The accuracy improves with better audio quality and proper enunciation.

Can I edit the generated blog post?

Absolutely! Our built-in editor allows you to customize, refine, and perfect your content. You have full control over the final output.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

How long does the conversion take?

Processing time depends on video length. Typically, a 10-minute video takes 2-3 minutes to process. Longer videos may take up to 10 minutes.

Can I integrate with my existing CMS?

Yes! We offer direct integrations with WordPress, Medium, Ghost, Webflow, and more. You can also export in various formats (HTML, Markdown, Plain Text).
